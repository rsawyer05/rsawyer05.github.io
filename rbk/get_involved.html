Recycle Bikes For Kids

Donate





Money

Recycle Bikes For Kids relies on your donations to opperate. If you love what we do or think our program is fantastic please donate any amount you can spare.

100% of the porceeds go towards parts and bike shop operating costs to continue to give bikes to kids for free. Just click on the Donate button on this page. You don't need a Paypal account to donate.

Bikes

In order to give away 2000 bikes a year we need your old bikes and your neighbors old bikes (with permission). The bike can be any size or condition. We will fix it in our bike shop and give it to a deserving child.

Your Time

If you're willing to get your hands dirty and volunteer your time here at Recycle Bikes we would love to have you. Our bike shop has all the tools you need to learn to fix the kids bikes. Group volunteering is also available. Just contact Joe for details.
